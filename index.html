Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a referral?

No, you don't need a referral. You can book directly with us. Some insurance plans may require a referral for coverage — we recommend checking with your provider.

What should I wear?

Comfortable, loose-fitting clothing. For lower body issues, shorts are ideal; for upper body, a tank top or loose t-shirt works well.

How long is a session?

Initial consultations are 60 minutes, follow-ups 45 minutes. All sessions are one-on-one with your physiotherapist.

Do you accept insurance?

Yes, we are in-network with most major providers. We also offer direct billing. Contact us with your insurance details.

Cancellation policy?

Please notify us at least 24 hours in advance to avoid a cancellation fee. You can reschedule via our booking link or phone.
